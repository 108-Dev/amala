# Without You a Thousand Years
*Viraha-bhakti in the spirit of Narottama dasa Thakura*

**Voice:** Universal soul — nameless, timeless  
**Frame:** Amsterdam as Vrindavan. A moment without Him is a millennium.  
**Note:** Written in the *spirit* of Narottama dasa Thakura — not his vocabulary. The longing, not the idiom.

---

**[Suno prompt:]**
```
Devotional Indian classical ballad, meditative and cinematic, male lead vocals with longing and reverence, opens with harmonium and deep tanpura drone, tabla enters with intricate rhythms, sitar melodies lace the verses, layered call-and-response vocals in chorus, handclaps, bamboo flute flourishes, building emotional arc, spiritual longing and grief of separation, traditional bhajan textures meeting modern ballad structure, slow tempo, key stays constant, fade out on final chorus repetition
```

---

**[OPENING — tanpura drone, voice alone]**

*Jagannatha Svami...*
*nayana-patha-gami bhavatu me...*

---

**[VERSE 1]**

The water still moves through these canals, Lord
The bridges still hold and the grey sky stays
But something is missing from every morning
Something is gone from the light of the days

Without You a thousand years —
Without You a thousand years —
One moment away from Your lotus feet
And time itself disappears

---

**[VERSE 2]**

They say You are watching from Puri's shore
They say You are present in every heart
But Amsterdam stands at an empty altar
And what is together feels split apart

Without You a thousand years —
Without You a thousand years —
One moment away from Your lotus feet
And time itself disappears

---

**[BRIDGE — sitar solo, then voice returns softer]**

*Narottama wept in the night for his Lord*
*Called out across the river, called out across the dark*
*Amsterdam weeps in a different language*
*But the distance is the same — from the altar to the heart*

---

**[VERSE 3]**

We borrowed You back every summer, my Lord
We pulled Your chariot through the city streets
We dressed You and fed You and sang to You
Then sent You back to Belgium in the heat

Without You a thousand years —
Without You a thousand years —
One moment away from Your lotus feet
And time itself disappears

---

**[CLOSING — voice alone, then tanpura fades]**

*Jagannatha Svami...*
*nayana-patha-gami bhavatu me...*

O Lord of the Universe —
be the constant object of my vision.

*[fade on tanpura drone]*
