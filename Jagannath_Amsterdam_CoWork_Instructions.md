## 1. Project Identity & Mission

This is the working file for the devotional campaign to restore Lord Jagannath's permanent presence in Amsterdam, Netherlands. The campaign was initiated by His Holiness Maha Vishnu Swami and is being coordinated by Samuel Dirksz (Samkirtanaika-pitarau dasa).

### Core concept

Amsterdam is referred to as **Amsterdham** — Amsterdam as a spiritual dham. The campaign works on two parallel tracks:

- A formal GBC proposal for the return of the original Amsterdam Jagannath Deities (currently residing in Radhadesh, Belgium since 1981)
- A devotional music and media campaign to build congregation support and public awareness

### Historical anchor

Srila Prabhupada visited Amsterdam on **July 26, 1972**. He installed the original Jagannath Deities at the Amsterdam temple (Herengracht). A verified detail from Maha Vishnu Swami's direct voice message: during the visit, prasadam fell on the taxi floor — Prabhupada ate it without minding at all.

The Deities were carved in 1971, installed in 1972, moved to Radhadesh in 1981. Amsterdam has borrowed them back each summer for Ratha Yatra ever since.

### Campaign website

Domain: **jagannath-amsterdam.com** (purchased). To serve as campaign headquarters, archive, and media hub.

### WhatsApp group name

- Preferred: **Amsterdham Jagannath Seva** (service-oriented framing, GBC-appropriate)
- Alternative inner circle name: **Herengracht 1972**

## 3. Suno Production Principles

Hard-won learnings from production sessions — apply to every new prompt.

### Pronunciation
- "Jaya Jagannath" causes mispronunciation — replace with "Jagannatha Swami" throughout
- Phonetic respelling rarely fixes Sanskrit — restructuring the phrase works better
- Adding a natural cadence landing word (like "Swami") after the name gives the phrase space to resolve correctly

### Anti-Bollywood production
- Always state explicitly: "no orchestration, no strings, no Bollywood production"
- Specify: "dry sound, no reverb, no echo effects"
- Anchor in era: "inspired by Srila Prabhupada kirtan recordings 1960s 1970s"
- Suno defaults to cinematic production unless explicitly told not to

### Sanskrit integrity
- Sanskrit texts (especially Jagannath Ashtakam) are kept fully intact
- Music is built around Sanskrit — never substituted for it
- Instruct Suno: "the Sanskrit verses are the anchor — the music builds around them not over them"

## 4. Campaign Strategy

### Framing principles
- Service-oriented, not political — all materials framed as devotional seva, not a challenge to any authority
- GBC visibility is always a consideration — respectful language, no inflammatory framing
- Historical grounding — all factual claims traceable to verified sources
- Maha Vishnu Swami's voice messages are the primary source for the 1972 prasadam detail

### Ratha Yatra route
Amsterdam Ratha Yatra: **De Pijp → Dam Square**. This is the specific route referenced in Pull! Jagannatha Swami! and in all campaign materials.

### GBC proposal
Formal GBC proposal in development. Public campaign builds congregation-level support as evidence of genuine desire for the Deities' return — not just one or two individuals.

### What the campaign is NOT doing
- Not attacking Radhadesh or any temple
- Not moving without proper GBC process
- Not making claims beyond verified sources
- Not using aggressive or confrontational language in any public-facing material
