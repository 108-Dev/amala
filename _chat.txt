As you know, these Deities were personally installed by Śrīla Prabhupāda, and therefore they carry immense historical, spiritual, and emotional significance. They are part of Śrīla Prabhupāda’s direct legacy, and it is our collective responsibility to preserve and properly honor that heritage.

We would therefore like to respectfully request that you, as GBC representatives, consider facilitating a careful and open evaluation of the possibility of returning the original Lord Jagannath, Baladeva, and Subhadra Deities to Amsterdam.

We also wish to express that:

- We are not requesting replicas for Amsterdam; rather, we are hoping for the return of the original Deities installed by Śrīla Prabhupāda.
- Replicas could be created for the Radhadesh community, allowing them to continue their worship with appropriate outfits harmonized with their existing Deities of Radha-Krishna and Gaura-Nitai.
- In Amsterdam, the worship standards for Lord Jagannath, Baladeva, and Subhadra would be properly aligned with traditional Jagannath worship standards, which differ from Radha-Krishna worship and can be established accordingly.
- The Amsterdam community is prepared to gradually develop suitable outfits and infrastructure for Their Lordships, understanding that this can be done steadily and with care.

We kindly request your guidance, support, and leadership in exploring this matter further. We approach this request with humility and respect, recognizing the sensitivities involved, and with the shared desire to serve Śrīla Prabhupāda’s mission in the best possible way. Thank you very much for your time, consideration, and service.
